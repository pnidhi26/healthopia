Regenerative Medicine (Stem Cell)


Regenerative medicine is aimed at treating a wide range of conditions by helping the body repair tissue or organs that are diseased, dysfunctional or injured. When tissue or organs are damaged, they could potentially repair themselves with these pluripotent stem cells; this process could reverse or prevent damage to vital organs. This phenomenon already occurs in nature. The zebrafish, a native to South East Asia, can , even as an adult.
